Technical Analysis for Beginners

Technical analysis is just reading a stock's price chart to spot what buyers and sellers are doing — no accounting degree required. Instead of studying a company's profits, you learn to read the "footprints" that price and volume leave behind, like OGDC on the PSX or Apple on the Nasdaq. This hub starts from absolute zero: by the end you'll be able to open any chart, understand what you're looking at, and make calmer entry and exit decisions.
